This brief description is what will describe the functionality of your newsfeed. Since almost all of your donors will be familiar with your organization by name and will be directed to download it from a link, the description can be very simple. We recommend something like the following:

“Enjoy real-time, relevant field updates directly from organization name’s newsfeed. Watch us as we work to insert brief description of what you do” (ex. …provide meals to the homeless, help the undeserved access medical care).
